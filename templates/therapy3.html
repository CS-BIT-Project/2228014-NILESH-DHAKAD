Causes of Poor Gut Health

Unhealthy gut can be caused by several factors:

  • Poor Diet: Processed and junk foods disrupt gut flora.
  • Stress: Mental stress negatively affects digestion.
  • Antibiotics: Frequent antibiotic use kills good bacteria.
  • Dehydration: Lack of water affects digestion and absorption.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Pawanmuktasana (Wind-Relieving Pose): Hold for 30 seconds.
  • Uttanpadasana (Raised Leg Pose): Hold for 20 seconds.
  • Dhanurasana (Bow Pose): Maintain for 30 seconds.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Kapalbhati: Perform for 5-10 minutes daily.
  • Bhastrika: 5 minutes in the morning.
  • Nadi Shodhana: 5 minutes daily.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Increase fiber intake with fruits & vegetables.
  • Consume probiotics like yogurt and fermented foods.
  • Drink warm lemon water every morning.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Reduce processed foods and refined sugar.
  • Drink plenty of water daily.
  • Exercise regularly to improve digestion.

Precautions

  • Avoid overeating and maintain portion control.
  • Do not perform yoga on a full stomach.
  • Consult a doctor for severe gut issues.
