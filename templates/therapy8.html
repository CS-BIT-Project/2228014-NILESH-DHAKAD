Causes of Poor Eye Vision

Vision problems can be caused by multiple factors:

  • Prolonged Screen Time: Leads to digital eye strain.
  • Nutritional Deficiencies: Lack of vitamins like A and E affects eyesight.
  • Aging: Natural decline in vision with age.
  • Lack of Eye Exercises: Weakens eye muscles.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Palming: Rub palms and place over eyes for relaxation.
  • Trataka (Candle Gazing): Enhances focus and vision.
  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Improves blood circulation to eyes.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Bhramari: Helps in reducing eye strain.
  • Anulom Vilom: Enhances oxygen supply to the eyes.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Consume Vitamin A-rich foods like carrots and leafy greens.
  • Include omega-3 fatty acids from nuts and seeds.
  • Stay hydrated to prevent dry eyes.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule (look 20 feet away every 20 minutes).
  • Reduce screen brightness and use anti-glare glasses.
  • Get adequate sleep for eye recovery.

Precautions

  • Avoid excessive use of digital screens.
  • Do not rub eyes excessively.
  • Consult an ophthalmologist for persistent issues.
