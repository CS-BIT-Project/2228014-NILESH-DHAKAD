Comprehensive Back Pain Therapy

Causes of Back Pain

  • Poor Posture: Sitting or standing incorrectly for long durations.
  • Muscle Strain: Overuse, heavy lifting, or sudden movements.
  • Sedentary Lifestyle: Lack of physical activity leading to weak muscles.
  • Obesity: Excess weight putting stress on the spine.
  • Medical Conditions: Herniated discs, arthritis, sciatica, or osteoporosis.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Hold for 20-30 seconds, repeat 3 times.
  • Marjariasana (Cat-Cow Pose): Perform for 1-2 minutes.
  • Balasana (Child's Pose): Hold for 30-60 seconds.
  • Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose): Hold for 20-30 seconds, repeat 3 times.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Anulom Vilom: 5-10 minutes daily.
  • Bhramari: 5-7 repetitions.
  • Kapalbhati: 3-5 minutes.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Increase intake of Calcium & Vitamin D (milk, yogurt, leafy greens).
  • Consume anti-inflammatory foods like turmeric, ginger, and nuts.
  • Avoid processed and sugary foods that increase inflammation.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a proper ergonomic posture while working.
  • Engage in regular physical activity like walking or swimming.
  • Sleep on a firm mattress for spinal support.

Precautions

  • Avoid lifting heavy objects improperly.
  • Do not perform yoga asanas forcefully.
  • Consult a doctor if pain persists for long durations.
