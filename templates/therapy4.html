Causes of Poor Sleep

Sleep issues can arise due to multiple reasons:

  • Stress & Anxiety: Overthinking prevents relaxation.
  • Poor Sleep Hygiene: Irregular sleep schedules.
  • Excess Screen Time: Blue light disrupts melatonin production.
  • Caffeine Intake: Stimulates the brain, making sleep difficult.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Consume warm milk with turmeric before bed.
  • Eat almonds and walnuts for better sleep.
  • Avoid heavy and spicy meals before bedtime.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Reduce screen time an hour before sleeping.
  • Engage in relaxing activities like reading or meditation.

Precautions

  • Avoid caffeine intake after evening.
  • Do not use your phone or laptop before sleeping.
  • Consult a doctor if insomnia persists.
