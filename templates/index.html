Calm Mind Therapy

This therapy helps relax the nervous system and reduce mental stress through calming yoga postures and breathwork. Gentle asanas and pranayama techniques improve emotional balance and promote inner peace.

Spinal Wellness

Designed to strengthen the spine and relieve back pain, this program includes targeted yoga postures and stretches. Regular practice improves flexibility, corrects posture, and reduces muscle stiffness.

Gut Cleanse Yoga

A therapeutic program to relieve indigestion, bloating, and constipation through yoga asanas and kriyas. Specific postures stimulate digestion, while pranayama enhances metabolism and gut health.

Deep Sleep

This therapy helps improve sleep quality by calming the mind and relaxing the body. Gentle yoga postures, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques reduce stress and promote deep rest.
