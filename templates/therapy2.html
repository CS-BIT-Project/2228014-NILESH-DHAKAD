Causes of Mental Stress

The mind becomes restless due to various reasons, including:

  • Overthinking: Continuous negative or anxious thoughts.
  • Lack of Sleep: Insufficient rest affecting mental peace.
  • Poor Diet: Junk food and caffeine increasing stress levels.
  • Unmanaged Workload: Excess responsibilities causing mental fatigue.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Yoga and Pranayama help in calming the mind. Below are effective practices:

Asanas (Postures)

  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Lie down and relax for 5-10 minutes.
  • Sukhasana (Easy Pose): Meditative posture for 10-15 minutes.
  • Balasana (Child's Pose): Hold for 30-60 seconds.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Anulom Vilom: 5-10 minutes daily.
  • Bhramari: 5-7 repetitions.
  • Nadi Shodhana: 5 minutes.

Dietary Recommendations

Eat foods that promote relaxation and mental clarity:

  • Consume fresh fruits like bananas and berries.
  • Drink herbal teas such as chamomile or ashwagandha.
  • Avoid excess caffeine and sugar.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Practice daily meditation for mental stability.
  • Reduce screen time before sleeping.
  • Engage in relaxing hobbies like reading or painting.

Precautions

  • Avoid overstimulating activities before bedtime.
  • Do not practice yoga forcefully, maintain comfort.
  • Seek professional help if anxiety persists.
