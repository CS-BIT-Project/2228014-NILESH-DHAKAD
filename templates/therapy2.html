Causes of Mental Stress

  • Overthinking: Continuous negative or anxious thoughts.
  • Lack of Sleep: Insufficient rest affecting mental peace.
  • Poor Diet: Junk food and caffeine increasing stress levels.
  • Unmanaged Workload: Excess responsibilities causing mental fatigue.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Yoga and Pranayama calm the nervous system and bring balance to the mind. Recommended practices include:

Asanas (Postures)

  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Lie down and relax for 5-10 minutes.
  • Sukhasana (Easy Pose): Meditative posture for 10-15 minutes.
  • Balasana (Child's Pose): Hold for 30-60 seconds.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Anulom Vilom: 5-10 minutes daily to balance nadis.
  • Bhramari: 5-7 repetitions for immediate relaxation.
  • Nadi Shodhana: Cleanses energy channels and calms the mind.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Consume fresh fruits like bananas and berries.
  • Drink herbal teas such as chamomile, tulsi, or ashwagandha.
  • Avoid stimulants like caffeine and excess sugar.

  • Limit screen time before sleeping for better melatonin production.
  • Engage in relaxing hobbies like music, art, journaling, or nature walks.

Precautions

  • Avoid overstimulating activities before bedtime.
  • Practice yoga and pranayama in a calm environment.
  • If anxiety continues, consult a yoga therapist or mental health expert.
