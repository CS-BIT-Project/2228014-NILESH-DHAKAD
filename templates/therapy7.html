Causes of Obesity

Obesity can be caused by multiple factors:

  • Poor Diet: Excessive consumption of processed and high-calorie foods.
  • Lack of Physical Activity: Sedentary lifestyle contributes to weight gain.
  • Genetics: Some people have a genetic predisposition to obesity.
  • Hormonal Imbalances: Conditions like hypothyroidism can lead to weight gain.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation): 12 rounds daily for weight management.
  • Trikonasana (Triangle Pose): Helps in reducing belly fat.
  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Boosts metabolism.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Kapalabhati: 5 minutes daily to burn calories.
  • Bhastrika: Increases metabolism and energy levels.
  • Anulom Vilom: Aids in balancing digestion.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Consume fiber-rich foods like vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.
  • Avoid sugary drinks and processed snacks.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Engage in daily physical activities like walking or yoga.
  • Maintain a balanced routine and manage stress effectively.
  • Get enough sleep to regulate metabolism.

Precautions

  • Avoid crash diets that may harm overall health.
  • Consult a nutritionist for a personalized diet plan.
  • Stay consistent with your exercise and yoga routine.
