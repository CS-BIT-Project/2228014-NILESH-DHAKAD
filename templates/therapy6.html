Stress Management Therapy

Introduction

Stress is a natural response to challenges, but chronic stress can lead to mental and physical health issues. Whether it's emotional pressure, work overload, or lack of rest, managing stress through yogic methods is highly effective.

Causes of Stress

  • Work Pressure: Deadlines and excessive workload.
  • Emotional Struggles: Anxiety, depression, and overthinking.
  • Unhealthy Lifestyle: Poor diet and lack of exercise.
  • Lack of Sleep: Reduces cognitive function and increases stress.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Incorporating specific yoga asanas and pranayama practices can bring calmness, clarity, and emotional resilience.

Asanas (Postures)

  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Relaxes the mind and body deeply.
  • Vrikshasana (Tree Pose): Helps improve focus and stability.
  • Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend): Calms the nervous system and promotes introspection.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Bhramari: 5 repetitions for instant calm and relaxation.
  • Nadi Shodhana: 5 minutes of alternate nostril breathing for balance.
  • Sheetali: Cooling breath to reduce internal heat and stress.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Eat magnesium-rich foods like spinach and almonds.
  • Include calming herbal teas such as chamomile and ashwagandha.
  • Avoid processed foods, refined sugar, and caffeine.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Develop a consistent meditation routine.
  • Walk in nature and engage in physical activities regularly.
  • Practice journaling and express gratitude daily.
