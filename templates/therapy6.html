Stress Management Therapy

Causes of Stress

Stress can be caused by multiple factors:

  • Work Pressure: Deadlines and excessive workload.
  • Emotional Struggles: Anxiety, depression, and overthinking.
  • Unhealthy Lifestyle: Poor diet and lack of exercise.
  • Lack of Sleep: Reduces cognitive function and increases stress.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Relaxes the mind and body.
  • Vrikshasana (Tree Pose): Improves focus and balance.
  • Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend): Calms the nervous system.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Bhramari: 5 repetitions for immediate relaxation.
  • Nadi Shodhana: 5 minutes to balance energy levels.
  • Sheetali: Cooling breath to reduce stress.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Consume magnesium-rich foods like spinach and almonds.
  • Include herbal teas such as chamomile and ashwagandha.
  • Avoid processed foods and excessive caffeine.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a consistent meditation routine.
  • Engage in regular physical activity and nature walks.
  • Practice gratitude and journaling to reduce mental clutter.

Precautions

  • Avoid overloading yourself with unnecessary commitments.
  • Seek professional help if stress becomes overwhelming.
  • Ensure a balanced routine with sufficient rest.
