Causes of Blood Sugar Imbalance

Blood sugar levels can be affected by several factors:

  • Poor Diet: High intake of processed sugar and refined carbs.
  • Lack of Exercise: Reduces insulin sensitivity.
  • Stress & Poor Sleep: Increases cortisol, leading to spikes in sugar levels.
  • Dehydration: Affects kidney function and blood sugar regulation.

Yogic Therapy Cure

Asanas (Postures)

  • Dhanurasana (Bow Pose): Enhances pancreas function.
  • Halasana (Plow Pose): Improves metabolism.
  • Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Spinal Twist): Helps in insulin regulation.

Pranayama (Breathing Exercises)

  • Kapalbhati: 5 minutes for pancreas stimulation.
  • Bhastrika: Boosts oxygen supply and metabolism.
  • Anulom Vilom: Balances hormones.

Dietary Recommendations

  • Include fiber-rich foods like vegetables and whole grains.
  • Consume cinnamon and fenugreek for better glucose control.
  • Avoid sugary beverages and processed food.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a regular exercise routine.
  • Stay hydrated and manage stress levels.
  • Follow a consistent sleep schedule.

Precautions

  • Avoid excessive consumption of high glycemic index foods.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly.
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist.
